A Visit to the UW

Mar 28th, 2013

Recently Julep was invited to the University of Washington to present to the Computer Science students on life in a startup. We were impressed with the number of students present and the lively conversation which ensued. While Julep has been around as a Nail Parlor for some time, the e-commerce solution will celebrate only it’s 2nd birthday this coming summer.

More intriguing, beginning with myself, the entire engineering team has a tenure of just over six months. In a very short time we’ve written a lot of code and evanglized the merits of Lean Agile developement. In a follow up post, I’ll talk more about our software development process. We hail from 3 distinct geographies: Seattle, Michigan and Ukraine.
